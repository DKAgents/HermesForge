# Risk Guardian Decision Log

## Purpose
Every formal Risk Guardian decision is logged here — approvals, rejections, conditionals, and escalations. This creates an auditable record of all risk decisions.

## Decision Template
```
### GD-XXX: [Title]
- Date: YYYY-MM-DD
- Submitted by: [agent or human]
- Type: Strategy Review | Position Review | Rule Change | Escalation
- Decision: ✅ APPROVED | ❌ REJECTED | ⚠️ CONDITIONAL | 🔺 ESCALATED
- Conditions (if conditional): 
- Rationale: 
- Human approval required: Yes/No
- Human approval received: Yes/No/Pending
```
