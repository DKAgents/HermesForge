# STARC Bands — Buy Signal at Lower Band

When prices dip to or below the starc- band, this represents a low-risk buying opportunity and a high-risk time to sell. In the gold example, prices touching the starc- band multiple times (points 1, 3, 4) led to stabilization or higher prices within 1–2 weeks.
