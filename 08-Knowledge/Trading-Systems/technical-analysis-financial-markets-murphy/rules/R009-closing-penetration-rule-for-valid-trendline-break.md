# Closing Penetration Rule for Valid Trendline Break

As a general rule, a close beyond the trendline is more significant than just an intraday penetration. A closing penetration provides a stronger signal of a genuine trendline break. Intraday violations alone are generally insufficient to confirm a trendline break.
