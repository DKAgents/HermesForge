# 10-Day Moving Average of Arms Index – Oversold/Overbought Levels

Most traders use a 10-day moving average of the Arms Index rather than its raw daily value. A 10-day average above 1.20 is considered oversold (bullish signal), while a value below 0.70 is considered overbought (bearish signal). These threshold numbers may shift depending on the overall trend of the market.
