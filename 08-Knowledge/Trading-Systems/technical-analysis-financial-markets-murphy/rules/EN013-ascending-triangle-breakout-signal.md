# Ascending Triangle Breakout Signal

A bullish breakout from an ascending triangle is signaled by a decisive closing above the flat upper trendline, accompanied by a noticeable increase in volume. A return move back to the support line (the flat upper line) is not unusual and should occur on light volume.


## Direct Quotes

> "The bullish breakout is signaled by a decisive closing above the flat upper trendline." — John J. Murphy

> "As in the case of all valid upside breakouts, volume should see a noticeable increase on the breakout." — John J. Murphy
