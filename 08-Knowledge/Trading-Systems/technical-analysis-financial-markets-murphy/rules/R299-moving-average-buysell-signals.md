# Moving Average Buy/Sell Signals

Moving averages are trend-following indicators that work best in trending environments but operate with a time lag. When only one average is employed, a buy signal is given when the price closes above the average. When two averages are employed, a buy signal is given when the shorter average crosses above the longer average. There are three types: simple, weighted, and exponentially smoothed averages.
