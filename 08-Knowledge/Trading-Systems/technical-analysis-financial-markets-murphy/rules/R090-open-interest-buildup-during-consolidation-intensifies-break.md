# Open Interest Buildup During Consolidation Intensifies Breakout

If open interest builds up noticeably during a sideways consolidation or horizontal trading range, the ensuing price move intensifies once the breakout occurs. Traders caught on the wrong side of the breakout are forced to liquidate, adding fuel to the new trend. The greater the increase in open interest during a trading range or price formation, the greater the potential for the subsequent price move.


## Direct Quotes

> "the greater the increase in open interest during a trading range (or any price formation for that matter), the greater the potential for the subsequent price move" — John J. Murphy
