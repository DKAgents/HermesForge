# Equivolume Chart Bullish Breakout Rule

A bullish price breakout should always be accompanied by a burst of trading activity. On an Equivolume chart, this is represented by a noticeably wider rectangle. In an uptrend, up days should show wider rectangles while down days should show narrower rectangles.


## Direct Quotes

> "a bullish price breakout should always be accompanied by a burst of trading activity" — John J. Murphy
