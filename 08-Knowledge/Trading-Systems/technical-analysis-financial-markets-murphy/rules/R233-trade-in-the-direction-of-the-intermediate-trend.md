# Trade in the Direction of the Intermediate Trend

Traders should always trade in the direction of the intermediate trend. In uptrends, buy the dips; in downtrends, sell bounces. This aligns trades with the dominant market direction and improves the probability of success.


## Direct Quotes

> "Trade in the direction of the intermediate trend." — John J. Murphy

> "In uptrends, buy the dips; in downtrends, sell bounces." — John J. Murphy
