# Time Factor in Triangles

Triangles are considered intermediate patterns, typically taking longer than one month but less than three months to form. A triangle lasting less than a month is likely a different pattern, such as a pennant.


## Direct Quotes

> "The triangle is considered an intermediate pattern, meaning that it usually takes longer than a month to form, but generally less than three months." — John J. Murphy

> "A triangle that lasts less than a month is probably a different pattern, such as a pennant." — John J. Murphy
