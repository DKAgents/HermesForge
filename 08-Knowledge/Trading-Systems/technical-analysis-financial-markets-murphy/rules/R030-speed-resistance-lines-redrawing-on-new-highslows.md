# Speed Resistance Lines — Redrawing on New Highs/Lows

Each time a new high is set in an uptrend or a new low in a downtrend, a new set of speedlines must be drawn because there is now a new high or low point. Because speedlines are drawn from the beginning of the trend through the one-third and two-thirds points, those trendlines may sometimes move through some of the price action.
