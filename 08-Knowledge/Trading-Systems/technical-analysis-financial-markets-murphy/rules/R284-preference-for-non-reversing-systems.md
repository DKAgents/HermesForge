# Preference for Non-Reversing Systems

Systems that do not reverse automatically are preferred; it is better to exit a trade first before putting on another trade in the opposite direction. This approach provides more control over position management and avoids the risks of automatic reversal systems.


## Direct Quotes

> "I prefer systems that do not reverse automatically—I like to exit a trade first, before putting on another trade in the opposite direction." — John J. Murphy
