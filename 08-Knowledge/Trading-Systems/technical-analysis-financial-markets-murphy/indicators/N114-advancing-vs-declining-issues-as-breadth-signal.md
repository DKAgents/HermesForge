# Advancing vs. Declining Issues as Breadth Signal

When there are more declining stocks than advancing stocks on a given day, market breadth is negative even if the Dow closes higher. Similarly, more declining volume than advancing volume reinforces a negative breadth reading. These figures provide a more accurate picture of market health than the Dow alone.


## Direct Quotes

> "there were more declining stocks (1,559) than advancing stocks (1,327), suggesting that the broader market didn't fare as well as the Dow." — John J. Murphy
