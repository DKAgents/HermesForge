# Rate of Change Oscillator Construction

Rate of change employs a price ratio over a selected span of time to construct an overbought-oversold oscillator. To construct a 10-day rate of change oscillator, the last closing price is divided by the closing price 10 days earlier. The resulting value is plotted above or below a value of 100.


## Direct Quotes
