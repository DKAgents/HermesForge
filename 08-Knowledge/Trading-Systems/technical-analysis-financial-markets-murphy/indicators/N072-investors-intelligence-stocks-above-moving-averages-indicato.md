# Investors Intelligence Stocks Above Moving Averages Indicator

Investors Intelligence publishes weekly figures measuring the percentage of stocks above their 10-week and 30-week moving averages. Readings above 70% suggest an overbought stock market; readings below 30% suggest an oversold market. The actual signal of a potential change in trend occurs when numbers rise back above 30 or fall back below 70. The 10-week readings are useful for short-to-intermediate turns; the 30-week numbers are better for major market turns.
