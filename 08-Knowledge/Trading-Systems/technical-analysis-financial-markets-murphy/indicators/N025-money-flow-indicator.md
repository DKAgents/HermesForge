# Money Flow Indicator

Developed by Laszlo Birinyi, Jr., Money Flow is a real-time version of OBV that tracks the level of volume on each price change to determine if money is flowing into or out of a stock. It is facilitated by the availability of volume data for stocks on each price change during the day. This sophisticated calculation requires significant computer power and is not readily available to most traders.


## Direct Quotes
