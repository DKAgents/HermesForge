# STARC Bands as Trading Filters — High/Low Risk Zones

When prices are near or above the starc+ band, it is a high-risk time to buy and a low-risk time to sell. When prices are at or below the starc- band, it is a high-risk selling zone and a more favorable point to buy. These bands help traders avoid chasing the market, which almost always results in a poor entry price.


## Direct Quotes

> "When prices are near or above the starc+ band, it is a high risk time to buy and a low risk time to sell." — John J. Murphy

> "Conversely, if prices are at or below the starc- band, then it is a high risk selling zone and a more favorable point to buy." — John J. Murphy

> "Starc bands can help the trader avoid chasing the market, which almost always results in a poor entry price." — John J. Murphy
