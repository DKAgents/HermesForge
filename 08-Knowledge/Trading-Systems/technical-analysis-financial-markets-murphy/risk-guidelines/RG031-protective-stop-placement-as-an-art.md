# Protective Stop Placement as an Art

Protective stops are strongly recommended and their placement must combine technical chart factors with money management considerations. The trader must account for market volatility — more volatile markets require looser stops. Stops placed too close result in unwanted liquidation from short-term noise, while stops placed too far away result in larger losses. The goal is to find the right middle ground.


## Direct Quotes

> "Stop placement, however, is an art." — John J. Murphy

> "The more volatile the market is, the looser the stop that must be employed." — John J. Murphy
