# Commonly Used Moving Average Combinations

The most commonly used daily moving average combinations in futures markets are 4 and 9, 9 and 18, 5 and 20, and 10 and 40. Stock traders rely heavily on a 50-day (or 10-week) moving average. For longer-range stock market analysis, popular weekly moving averages are 30 and 40 weeks (or 200 days). Bollinger Bands use 20-day and 20-week moving averages, and the 20-week average can be approximated on daily charts with a 100-day average.
