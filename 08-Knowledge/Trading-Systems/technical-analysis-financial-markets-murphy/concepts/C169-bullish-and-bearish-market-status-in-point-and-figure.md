# Bullish and Bearish Market Status in Point and Figure

If the last signal was a buy signal, the position is bullish and remains bullish until a sell signal occurs. If the last signal was a sell-short signal, the position is bearish and remains bearish until a buy signal reversal occurs. This defines the ongoing market posture based on the most recent signal.


## Direct Quotes

> "BULLISH - If the last signal was a buy signal, the position is bullish and remains bullish until a sell signal" — John J. Murphy

> "BEARISH - If the last signal was a sell-short signal, the position is bearish and remains bearish until a buy reversal" — John J. Murphy
