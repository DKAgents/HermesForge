# Point and Figure Chart: Pure Price Movement

The point and figure chart is a study of pure price movement and does not take time into consideration while plotting price action. Only price changes are recorded; if no price change occurs, the chart is left untouched. During active market periods, considerable plotting is required; during quiet conditions, little or no plotting is needed.


## Direct Quotes

> "The point and figure chart is a study of pure price movement." — John J. Murphy

> "Only the price changes are recorded. If no price change occurs, the chart is left untouched." — John J. Murphy
