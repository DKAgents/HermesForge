# Constant Forward Continuous Contract

A Constant Forward Continuous Contract looks a constant length of time into the future using more than one contract. A common method uses the nearest two contracts and performs a linear extrapolation of the data to derive a synthetic price. This is also known as a Perpetual Contract and is used by Commodity Systems, Inc.


## Direct Quotes

> "A Constant Forward Continuous Contract looks a constant length of time into the future. It uses more than one contract to do this." — John J. Murphy
