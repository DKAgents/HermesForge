# Price Gaps Types

There are four main types of price gaps: common gaps, breakaway gaps (signal the start of a new trend), runaway/measuring gaps (occur in the middle of a trend and can be used to project price targets), and exhaustion gaps (signal the end of a trend). Island reversals form when an exhaustion gap is followed by a breakaway gap in the opposite direction.
