# Inverted Irregular Correction

An inverted irregular correction is a variant of the flat (3-3-5) corrective pattern where the internal wave structure is inverted relative to the standard irregular correction. It appears in both bull and bear market contexts and is classified as a corrective wave within the Elliott Wave framework.
