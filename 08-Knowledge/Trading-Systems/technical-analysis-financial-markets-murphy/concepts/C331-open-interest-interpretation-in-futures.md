# Open Interest Interpretation in Futures

Open interest is the number of options or futures contracts still unliquidated at the end of a trading day. A rise or fall in open interest shows that money is flowing into or out of a futures contract or option. In futures markets, rising open interest is considered good for the current trend, and open interest also measures liquidity.
