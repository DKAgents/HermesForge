# Falling Three Methods — Bearish Continuation Pattern

The Falling Three Methods is the bearish counterpart to the Rising Three Methods, occurring in a downtrend. It mirrors the Rising Three Methods structure but in the opposite direction, confirming continuation of a downtrend.
