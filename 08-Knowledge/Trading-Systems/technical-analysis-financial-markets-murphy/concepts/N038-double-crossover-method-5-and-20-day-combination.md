# Double Crossover Method: 5 and 20 Day Combination

The double crossover method uses two moving averages, with the 5 and 20 day combination being popular with futures traders. A sell signal is generated when the shorter average (5 day) falls below the longer average (20 day). This method can catch entire downtrends when the crossover occurs early in the move.
