# Intraday Point and Figure Chart Construction

The intraday point and figure chart was the original form of point and figure charting, designed to capture every one-point move in a stock. Each box is assigned a value (originally one point for stocks, adjusted for commodities), and fractions are ignored. The technique was intended to detect accumulation (buying) and distribution (selling) more effectively.


## Direct Quotes

> "The intent was to capture and record on paper each one point move of the stocks under consideration. It was felt that accumulation (buying) and distribution (selling) could be better detected in this manner." — John J. Murphy
