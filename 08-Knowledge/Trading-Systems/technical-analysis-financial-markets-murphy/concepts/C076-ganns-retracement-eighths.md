# Gann's Retracement Eighths

W.D. Gann broke down the trend structure into eighths — 1/8, 2/8, 3/8, 4/8, 5/8, 6/8, 7/8, 8/8. Even Gann attached special importance to the 3/8 (38%), 4/8 (50%), and 5/8 (62%) retracement numbers and also felt it was important to divide the trend into thirds — 1/3 (33%) and 2/3 (66%).
