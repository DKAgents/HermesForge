# Neural Networks in Intermarket Analysis

Neural networks provide a quantitative framework for identifying and tracking complex relationships among financial markets. They are used to detect hidden patterns and correlations between related markets that are difficult to identify manually. VantagePoint software, introduced in 1991, applies neural network technology to trade interest rate markets, stock indexes, currency markets, and energy futures.


## Direct Quotes

> "Neural networks provide a more quantitative framework for identifying and tracking the complex relationships that exist among the financial markets." — John J. Murphy
