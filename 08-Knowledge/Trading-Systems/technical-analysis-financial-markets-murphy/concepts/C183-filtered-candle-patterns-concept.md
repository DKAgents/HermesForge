# Filtered Candle Patterns — Concept

Candle pattern filtering, developed by Greg Morris in 1991, improves the overall reliability of candle patterns by combining them with traditional technical oscillators to identify overbought and oversold conditions. Only reversal candle patterns are considered under this concept, and patterns are only acted upon when the oscillator is in its presignal area (overbought or oversold zone). This technique helps eliminate bad or premature candle pattern signals.


## Direct Quotes

> "A revolutionary concept developed by Greg Morris in 1991, called candle pattern filtering, provides a simple method to improve the overall reliability of candle patterns." — John J. Murphy
