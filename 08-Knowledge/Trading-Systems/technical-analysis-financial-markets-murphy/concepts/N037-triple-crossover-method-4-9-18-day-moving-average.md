# Triple Crossover Method (4-9-18 Day Moving Average)

The most widely used triple crossover system uses 4, 9, and 18 day moving averages, primarily in futures trading. This concept was introduced by R.C. Allen in 1972. It is a variation on the 5, 10, and 20 day moving average numbers widely used in commodity circles. Many commercial chart services and charting software packages publish or default to the 4-9-18 day combination.
