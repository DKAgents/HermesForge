# Point and Figure X and O Columns

On a point and figure chart, alternating columns of x's and o's are used. X columns represent rising prices, while o columns show declining prices. Each time a column of x's moves one box above a previous column of x's, an upside breakout occurs; when a column of o's declines one box under a previous column of o's, a downside breakout occurs.


## Direct Quotes

> "The x columns represent rising prices, while the 0 columns show declining prices." — John J. Murphy
