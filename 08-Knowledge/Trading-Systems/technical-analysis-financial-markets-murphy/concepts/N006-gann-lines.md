# Gann Lines

Gann lines are trendlines drawn from prominent tops or bottoms at specific geometric angles. The most important Gann line is drawn at a 45-degree angle from a peak or trough. Steeper Gann lines can be drawn at 63⅓ and 75 degree angles during an uptrend, and flatter lines at 26⅔ and 15 degree angles. It is possible to draw as many as nine different Gann lines.
