# Fibonacci Numbers as Moving Averages

Fibonacci numbers such as 13, 21, 34, and 55 lend themselves well to moving average analysis on both daily and weekly charts. The 21-day moving average is a Fibonacci number, and the 13-week average has proven valuable in both stocks and commodities. These numbers appear to have a natural fit with market cycle analysis.
