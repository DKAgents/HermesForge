# Technical Analysis vs Fundamental Analysis

The technician does not concern themselves with the reasons why prices rise or fall, particularly in early trend stages or at critical turning points where the reasons are often unknown. The chartist lets the market indicate direction rather than trying to outsmart or outguess it. Technicians indirectly study fundamentals through price, since price reflects supply and demand forces.


## Direct Quotes

> "As a rule, chartists do not concern themselves with the reasons why prices rise or fall." — John J. Murphy, p. unknown

> "The chartist does not necessarily try to outsmart or outguess the market." — John J. Murphy, p. unknown

> "The chartist knows there are reasons why markets go up or down. He or she just doesn't believe that knowing what those reasons are is necessary in the forecasting process." — John J. Murphy, p. unknown
