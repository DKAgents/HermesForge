# Goal of Trading System Development

The goal of trading system development is not to develop a system that achieves the highest returns using historical data, but to formulate a sound concept that has performed reasonably well in the past and can be expected to continue to perform reasonably well in the future. The ideal approach is 100% mechanical, increasing the odds that past performance can be replicated in the future.


## Direct Quotes

> "Our goal is not to develop a system that achieves the highest returns using historical data, but to formulate a sound concept that has performed reasonably well in the past and can be expected to continue to perform reasonably well in the future." — John J. Murphy
