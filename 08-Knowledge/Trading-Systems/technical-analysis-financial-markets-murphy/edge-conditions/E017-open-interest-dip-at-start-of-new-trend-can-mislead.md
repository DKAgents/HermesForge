# Open Interest Dip at Start of New Trend Can Mislead

Because the impetus following the initial trend signal is often caused by those on the wrong side of the market liquidating, open interest sometimes dips slightly at the beginning of a new trend. This initial dip in open interest can mislead the unwary chart reader and argues against focusing too much attention on open interest changes over the very short term.
