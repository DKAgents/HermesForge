# Pennant Pattern Identification

A pennant is identified by two converging trendlines and is more horizontal in shape, closely resembling a small symmetrical triangle. It is a short-term continuation pattern that should be completed within one to three weeks. Volume should dry up noticeably while the pennant is forming.
