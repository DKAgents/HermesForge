# Island Reversal Pattern

The island reversal forms when, after an upward exhaustion gap, prices trade in a narrow range for a couple of days or weeks before gapping to the downside. This leaves the few days of price action looking like an 'island' surrounded by space. The exhaustion gap to the upside followed by a breakaway gap to the downside completes the island reversal and usually indicates a trend reversal of some magnitude.


## Direct Quotes
