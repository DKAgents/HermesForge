# Head and Shoulders Reversal Pattern

The head and shoulders is a major reversal pattern consisting of three peaks where the middle peak (head) is the highest, flanked by two lower peaks (shoulders). Volume confirmation, complex variations, failed patterns, and inverse formations are all important considerations when trading this pattern.
