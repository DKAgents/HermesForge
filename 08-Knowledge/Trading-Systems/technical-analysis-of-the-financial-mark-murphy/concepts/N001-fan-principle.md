# Fan Principle

After a trendline violation, prices may decline briefly before rallying back to the broken trendline (now resistance), forming a second trendline. After another failed rally, a third trendline is drawn. The breaking of the third trendline is the valid trend reversal signal. The lines gradually flatten out, resembling a fan. Previously broken support lines become resistance and resistance lines become support throughout this process.


## Direct Quotes

> "The breaking of that third trendline is usually an indication that prices are headed lower." — John J. Murphy
