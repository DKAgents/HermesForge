# Range Extension as Longer Term Trader Signal

Range extension occurs when longer term traders enter the market with enough volume to tip the initial balance and extend the range up or down. Range extension up indicates longer term buying while range extension down indicates longer term selling. On some occasions both longer term buyers and sellers are active at a range extreme but not at the same price and time, creating a situation where both kinds of activity occur in the same price area at different times.
