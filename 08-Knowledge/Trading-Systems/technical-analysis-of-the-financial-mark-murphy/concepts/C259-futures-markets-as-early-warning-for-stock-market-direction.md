# Futures Markets as Early Warning for Stock Market Direction

Early warning signs of inflation and interest rate trends are usually spotted in the futures pits first, which often determine the direction stock prices will take. Trends in the dollar reveal information about the strength or weakness of the American economy, which impacts corporate earnings and stock valuations. Sector rotation within the stock market is often dictated by action in futures markets.


## Direct Quotes
