# Double Bottom Pattern

The double bottom is the mirror image of the double top, forming in a downtrend with two prominent troughs at approximately the same price level. The measuring technique is the same as the double top but applied in the opposite direction. A break of a down trendline can confirm the upturn after a double bottom forms.
