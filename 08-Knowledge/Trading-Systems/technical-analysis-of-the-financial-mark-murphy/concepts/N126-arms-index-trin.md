# Arms Index (TRIN)

The Arms Index, created by Richard Arms, is a ratio of ratios: the numerator is advancing issues divided by declining issues, and the denominator is advancing volume divided by declining volume. A reading below 1.0 indicates more volume in rising stocks and is positive; a reading above 1.0 reflects more volume in declining issues and is negative. It is a contrary indicator that trends in the opposite direction of the market.
