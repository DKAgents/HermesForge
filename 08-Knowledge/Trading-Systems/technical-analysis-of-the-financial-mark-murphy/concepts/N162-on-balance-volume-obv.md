# On Balance Volume (OBV)

Developed by Joseph Granville, OBV is a running cumulative total of upside and downside volume. Volume is added on up days and subtracted on down days. The OBV line is plotted alongside the price line to see if the two lines are confirming each other.


## Direct Quotes

> "Volume is added on up days and subtracted on down days. The OBV line is plotted with the price line to see if the two lines are confirming each other." — John J. Murphy
