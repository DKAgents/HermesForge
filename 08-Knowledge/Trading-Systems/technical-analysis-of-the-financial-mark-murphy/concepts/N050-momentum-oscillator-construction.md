# Momentum Oscillator Construction

Momentum measures the velocity of price changes rather than actual price levels. A 10-day momentum line is constructed by subtracting the closing price 10 days ago from the latest closing price. The resulting positive or negative value is plotted around a zero line. The formula is M = V - V*, where V is the latest closing price and V* is the closing price x days ago.


## Direct Quotes

> "Momentum measures the velocity of price changes as opposed to the actual price levels themselves." — John J. Murphy

> "M=V-Vv*" — John J. Murphy
