# Initial Balance Established in First Hour

A market's initial balance — a place where two-sided trade can occur — is usually established in the first hour of trade by short term buyers and sellers (day timeframe activity) in their pursuit of a fair price. Most of the day's activity occurs in the fair price or value area. Prices above and below this developed fair value area offer opportunity and are advantageous to longer term traders.
