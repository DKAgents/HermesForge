# Simple Moving Average

The simple moving average, or arithmetic mean, is the type used by most technical analysts. It gives equal weight to each day's price — in a 10-day average, each day receives 10% weighting; in a 5-day average, each day receives 20% weighting. Critics argue it only accounts for the period covered by the average and gives equal weight to all days regardless of recency.
