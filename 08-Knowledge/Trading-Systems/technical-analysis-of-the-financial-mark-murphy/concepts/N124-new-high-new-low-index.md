# New High-New Low Index

The New High-New Low Index plots the difference between the number of stocks hitting new highs and new lows, and can be directly compared to major market averages just like an advance-decline line. A new high in the Dow not matched by a corresponding new high in the high-low line signals weakness in the broader market. Dr. Alexander Elder describes it as 'probably the best leading indicator of the stock market.' Trendline and moving-average analysis can be applied to the line.
