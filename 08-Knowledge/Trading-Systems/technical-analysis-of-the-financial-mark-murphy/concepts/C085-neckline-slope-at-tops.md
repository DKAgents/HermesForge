# Neckline Slope at Tops

The neckline drawn under the two reaction lows of a head and shoulders top generally has a slight upward slope. It is sometimes horizontal and less often tilts downward. The slope of the neckline can provide additional context about the pattern's character.


## Direct Quotes

> "This line generally has a slight upward slope at tops (although it's sometimes horizontal and, less often, tilts downward)." — John J. Murphy
