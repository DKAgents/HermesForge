# Maximum Entropy Spectral Analysis (MESA)

MESA is a statistical approach developed by John Ehlers for identifying dominant market cycles. Its main advantage is high-resolution measurement of cycles with relatively small time periods, which is crucial for shorter-term trading. It allows for dynamic adjusting of technical indicators to fit current market conditions.


## Direct Quotes
