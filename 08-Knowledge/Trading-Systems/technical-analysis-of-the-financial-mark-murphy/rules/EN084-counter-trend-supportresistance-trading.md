# Counter Trend: Support/Resistance Trading

A counter-trend approach based on support and resistance involves buying a decline into support and selling a rally into resistance. This method fades the prevailing short-term move by anticipating a reversal at known price levels.


## Direct Quotes

> "Buy a decline into support; sell a rally into resistance." — John J. Murphy
