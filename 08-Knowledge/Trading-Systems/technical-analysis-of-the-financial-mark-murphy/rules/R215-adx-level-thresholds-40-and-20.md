# ADX Level Thresholds — 40 and 20

When the ADX line starts to drop from above the 40 level, that is an early sign that the trend is weakening. A rise back above the 20 level is often a sign of the start of a new trend. A downturn from above 40 signals the onset of a trading range, while an upturn from below 20 signals the resumption of a trending phase.
