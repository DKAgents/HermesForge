# Channel Line as Profit-Taking Zone

The upper channel line in an uptrend can be used for short-term profit taking on long positions. When price reaches the upper parallel channel line, traders holding long positions initiated near the basic trendline can use this level to exit or reduce exposure. This technique leverages the tendency of prices to oscillate between the two parallel boundaries of a channel.
