# Three Elements of Successful Trading

Any successful trading program must account for three important factors: price forecasting, timing (trading tactics), and money management. No trading program can be considered complete without all three elements working together. Price forecasting alone is insufficient without proper timing and capital management.


## Direct Quotes

> "Any successful trading program must take into account three important factors: price forecasting, timing, and money management." — John J. Murphy

> "No trading program can be complete without all three elements." — John J. Murphy
